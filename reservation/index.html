About Aura Bay

Your sanctuary in the tropics

Nestled on the pristine shores of a private island, Aura Bay Resort offers an unparalleled luxury experience. Our commitment to sustainable tourism and authentic local culture creates a harmonious blend of modern comfort and natural beauty. Whether you seek adventure in our lush rainforests or tranquility on our white sand beaches, every moment at Aura Bay is designed to rejuvenate your spirit.
